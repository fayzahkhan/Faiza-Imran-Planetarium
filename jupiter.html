Jupiter

King of the Planets

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System, with a mass 2.5 times that of all other planets combined. It is a gas giant composed primarily of hydrogen and helium, featuring a rapid rotation that completes a day in just 9.9 hours and an orbital period of 11.86 Earth years.

Diameter: 139,820 km

Moons: 95 Confirmed
