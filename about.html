SYSTEM: PLANETARIUM

About The Experience

Big Planetarium is a digital exploration system designed to make the universe feel accessible, interactive, and personal. Instead of just reading about space, users can experience it — from exploring exoplanets to discovering what the sky looked like on their birthday.

IMMERSION

Designed with a futuristic interface, the system blends minimalism with atmospheric visuals to create a calm but powerful space experience.

PURPOSE

This project exists to make complex space data simple, visual, and engaging — turning curiosity into exploration.

“To bring the universe closer — not as information, but as an experience.”
